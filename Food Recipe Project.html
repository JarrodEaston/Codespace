Ingredients and how to make it

ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • 2 hamburgers

    • How to make it

    • Get 1 large egg and cook it in a pan
    • Take out 4 hamburger buns
    • Take 2 hamburgers and cook them in the frying pan after the egg is finished
    • Once the burgers are done, take them out on put them on the burger buns.
    • Put ketchup and mustard on the burgers
    • Enjoy!
